<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Evolution Quest Walkthrough">

Completing the evolution quest requires a mix of general map clears, target kill farming, and game mode participation. The tracking system updates in real-time, allowing you to monitor your objectives between matches. Follow these steps to complete the quest with maximum efficiency:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Base Unit">
    Ensure that Elastic Captain (Pirate) is slotted into your active loadout. The quest objectives will not track any progress if the unit remains in your inventory during matches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Target Kills in Story Mode">
    You must register a total of 5,000 kills using Elastic Captain. Load into low-difficulty acts with high enemy density, such as the early stages of Planet Namak or Double Dungeon. Deploy Elastic Captain early to ensure he secures the final blows on incoming waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Pirate Dynasty Stages">
    Complete 10 successful runs of stages associated with the Pirate Dynasty theme. These maps feature specialized modifiers that favor units with high placement reach and rapid attack speeds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Fulfill Rift Requirements">
    Participate in and clear 5 active Rift encounters. Rifts spawn randomly in the lobby or can be accessed via player-hosted portals. Working with a coordinated team ensures fast clears under strict time limits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Objective | Target Requirement | Recommended Farming Stage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Enemy Defeats** | 5,000 Kills | Planet Namak (Act 1 or Act 3) |
| **Theme Clears** | 10 Successful Runs | Pirate Dynasty Stages |
| **Rift Victories** | 5 Rift Clearances | Active Rift Portals |
| **Gold Investment** | 50,000 Gold | Daily Challenges / Boss Rush |

<Callout type="tip" title="Slowing Synergies">
Pair Elastic Captain with crowd control units like Ice Queen (Release) or Gujo (Infinity) during your kill farming runs. Freezing or slowing enemies in his attack zone ensures he lands maximum hits and secures kills faster.
</Callout>

Avoid farming kills in endless modes or high-tier raids unless your unit is fully leveled. Low-level base units struggle to secure kills when paired with high-damage teammates, which drastically slows down your quest progression.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Cog 5th Performance & Best Trait Setup">

Upgrading to Elastic Captain (Cog 5th) completely transforms the unit's combat profile. He shifts from a localized single-target attacker to a premier AoE lane-clearing monster. His final upgrade path introduces massive circular attacks that knock back enemies and apply progressive damage-over-time effects.

To maximize this damage potential, you must roll for optimal traits using the Mandra Traits NPC in the lobby. Because Cog 5th has a low placement limit, you should focus on traits that provide massive individual stat multipliers rather than utility boosts.

| Stat Metric | Base Form (Pirate) | Evolved Form (Cog 5th) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage Output** | Moderate | Extremely High (S-Tier scaling) |
| **Attack Type** | Single-Target / Small Cone | Massive Circular AoE |
| **Placement Limit** | 4 Units | 2 Units |
| **Special Utility** | Knockback | Burn Damage + Knockback |

<Callout type="success" title="Ideal Trait Selection">
Roll for **Monarch** (0.1% chance) or **Ethereal** (0.175% chance) as your primary endgame targets. Because you can only place two Cog 5th units on the map, the massive raw damage multiplier of Monarch easily outweighs the placement restriction.
</Callout>

If you cannot secure a mythic trait immediately, settle for **Blitz** (1.85% chance) or **Deadeye** (0.375% chance). These options drastically reduce the unit's attack cooldown (SPA), allowing him to chain his knockback abilities and permanently stall heavy waves.

</Section>
