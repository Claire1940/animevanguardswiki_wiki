<Section icon={Target} title="Steps to Complete the Zoro Evolution Quest">

Unlike standard units that only require materials, the **anime vanguards zoro evo** route is locked behind an active Evolution Questline. You must complete these challenges with the base unit equipped in your active loadout to register progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Base Bounty Hunter">
    Place your base Bounty Hunter unit into your active team loadout. Quest progress will not track if the unit is sitting in your inventory during matches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat 5,000 Enemies">
    Enter any high-density game mode, such as Story Mode or Infinite Mode, and use Bounty Hunter to land the finishing blows. Stacking slow or stun units helps bunch enemies together for easy slash kills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Pirate Dynasty Stages">
    Successfully complete 15 runs of the Pirate Dynasty gamemode on Hard difficulty or higher. This step also grants you a chance to drop the rare Green Bandana material.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assemble Materials at the Evolve Station">
    Once all quests are marked complete, head to the Upgrades area in the main lobby. Interact with the Evolve NPC, select Bounty Hunter, and pay the required Gold and materials to unlock the King of Hell form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Quest Tracking Tip">
You can monitor your live quest progress by opening the Quest tab on the left side of the screen while in the lobby. If your kills are not registering, double-check that Bounty Hunter is landing the final hits rather than your support or burn units.
</Callout>
