### SS-Tier Meta Cores

These units are the foundation of any successful endgame roster. They excel in Raids, Rifts, and high-floor Worldlines progression.

- **Song Jinwu and Igros**: This Vanguard-rarity unit dominates the meta because it summons shadow soldiers onto the track. These shadow units fight enemies directly without consuming your valuable normal placement slots, providing massive frontline pressure.
- **Gujo (Infinity)**: A legendary crowd-control DPS. Gujo uses wide-area stuns and slows to hold fast-moving enemy waves in place, allowing your primary damage dealers to maximize their DPS windows.
- **Ice Queen (Release)**: The premier freeze-control unit. Evolving her unlocks massive wave-locking capabilities, making her a staple for stall-heavy strategies in infinite modes.
- **Alocard (Vampire King)**: A bleed-focused DPS powerhouse. Alocard excels on multi-lane maps where his stacking bleed status effect melts high-health targets over time.

<Section icon={Map} title="Team Building & Progression Strategy">

Building a balanced team is far more effective than simply stacking your highest-rarity damage dealers. A proper team needs a mixture of economy, crowd control, and sustained DPS.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Your Economy Support">
    Start your setup by deploying cost-reduction or income-generating units like Demon Leader. This ensures you have enough gold to upgrade your primary carries during long waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish Lane Control">
    Place a crowd-control unit such as Gujo (Infinity) or Ice Queen (Release) near critical chokepoints. Slowing or freezing enemy waves prevents fast enemies from leaking past your defenses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Deploy Your Main Carry">
    Bring out your heavy hitters like Rogita (Super 4) or Today's Strongest vs History's Strongest to melt boss targets and clear dense waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Summon Pressure">
    Fill any remaining gaps with slotless summoners like Song Jinwu and Igros to create a reliable frontline buffer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Progression Tip">
Always check your unit's stats and traits before entering high-difficulty Raids. A poorly rolled trait on a top-tier unit can significantly lower your team's overall combat effectiveness.
</Callout>
