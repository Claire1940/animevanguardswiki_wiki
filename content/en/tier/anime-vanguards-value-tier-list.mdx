<Section icon={Target} title="How to Maximize Unit Value: Evolution and Ascension">

Evolving and ascending your characters is the most direct path to increasing their overall value. Evolution not only boosts base stats but often completely transforms a unit's attack patterns, passive abilities, and overall utility. Ascension, on the other hand, is a long-term endgame system that further refines your Mythic units through targeted stat boosts.

Follow this systematic approach to plan your upgrades efficiently, preventing you from wasting gold, essence stones, and duplicate characters on units you will eventually replace.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Your Final Unit First">
    Identify which units are carrying your Story, Legend, Raid, Rift, Odyssey, Worldlines, or Boss Rush teams. Prioritize evolving characters that remain useful across multiple game modes rather than niche, single-purpose units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Required Materials">
    Evolution requirements vary wildly. Check the Evolve station to see which specific essence stones, gold, and unit-specific drop items are needed. Avoid partial investments; save up until you can complete the entire evolution in one go.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Evolution Quests">
    Major characters, such as the Pirate-themed units from Update 13.0 (e.g., Elastic Captain and Bounty Hunter), require you to finish specific multi-step Evolution Quests before they can be evolved. Track these quests in your log and complete them during normal gameplay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Evolution">
    Visit the Evolve station in the Upgrades area to finalize the process. Your unit will instantly gain increased stats, new attack animations, higher upgrade limits, and potentially a completely new role on your team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Ascend Only Long-Term Mythics">
    Ascension is handled through the Kokashi NPC and applies exclusively to Mythic units. It offers three distinct levels of stat upgrades. Ensure you do not spend duplicate copies on a unit unless you are certain it fits into your long-term account goals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
