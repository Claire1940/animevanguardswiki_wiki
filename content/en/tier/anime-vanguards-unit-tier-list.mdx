<Section icon={Shield} title="Evolution and Team Optimization Steps">

Evolving your units is the most reliable way to boost their ranking on the tier list. Evolution increases base statistics, unlocks stronger passives, and expands upgrade paths during battles. However, players must plan their upgrade route carefully to avoid wasting duplicates.

Follow these steps to optimize your roster and build a balanced endgame team:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Core Carry">
    Choose one high-damage carry, such as Rogita or God (Above Heaven), to act as your primary damage dealer across story and raid stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Evolution Materials">
    Collect essence stones, gold, and unit-specific drop items from Legend Stages and Raids. Avoid partial upgrades on temporary units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Evolve Before Ascending">
    Evolve your Mythic units to their final forms at the Evolve station. Do not ascend a Mythic unit if you plan to evolve it later, as the ascension levels will be removed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reroll Traits at Mandra NPC">
    Use Trait Rerolls on your evolved carry units. Aim for mythic traits like Monarch, Ethereal, or Deadeye to maximize their combat power.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Transfer Stats and Match Roles">
    Optimize the stats on your final units using Stat Chips. Ensure your support units carry traits like Fortune or Swift to assist your damage dealers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
