<Section icon={Shield} title="Evolution and Ascension Meta Progression">

Evolution is the primary method of increasing a unit's power ceiling. In the 2026 **anime vanguards meta**, simply having a Mythic unit is no longer enough; you must evolve them into their Secret or Vanguard forms to unlock their full potential.

| Upgrade Type | Rarity Requirement | Major Benefit | Meta Necessity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Evolution** | Any | New skills/passives | Mandatory |
| **Ascension** | Mythic Only | Flat stat bonuses | Optional/High-end |
| **Memoria** | All | Unique buffs/links | High |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Meta Targets">
    Choose units that define the current 13.5 meta, such as the Demon Hunter core or the new Summer units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Evolution Materials">
    Collect essence stones and gold. Use the **Legend Stages** and **Raids** to find unit-specific items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Evolution Quests">
    Units like **Elastic Captain** require specific multi-step quests. Finish these before attempting the upgrade area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ascend Mythic Units">
    Use the Kokashi NPC to add up to 10% extra damage via three levels of ascension. **Note**: Ascension is removed if a Mythic evolves into a higher rarity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Memoria Update">
Update 13.5 simplified the Memoria system. All Memoria are now evolved by default, and you can use **Memoria Recycling** to turn unwanted shards into **Memoria Scrap** for better upgrades.
</Callout>
