<Section icon={Shield} title="Evolution and Ascension Path">

Evolution transforms your units into stronger forms, while Ascension provides stat bonuses for Mythic units. Plan these upgrades carefully to avoid resource waste.

| Upgrade Type | Requirement | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Evolution | Materials + Quests | Stat boost, new passive, better abilities |
| Ascension I | Mythic + Copies | Damage +3% |
| Ascension II | Mythic + Copies | Range +5% |
| Ascension III | Mythic + Copies | Damage +7% |

<Callout type="success" title="Resource Strategy">
Always complete the rarity-changing evolution first. Ascension bonuses are lost when a unit evolves into a higher rarity, so save those resources until you have your final unit form.
</Callout>
