Acquiring an **anime vanguards shiny** unit requires a combination of luck and persistent grinding. While summoning remains the most common method, high-tier Portals and special events like the Odyssey offer guaranteed paths to the items that make these units truly overpowered.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summoning and Portal Grinding">
    Begin by targeting specific banners or grinding Tier 4 Portals. Secret Portals have a 1% drop rate for Vanguard capsules, which are essential for obtaining the Familia items that buff Shiny units by up to 200% damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stat Chip Accumulation">
    Collect Stat Chips from daily tasks and portal clears. You will need thousands of these to hit "Godly" stats. A maxed Shiny unit typically requires a 9,000+ Stat Chip reserve to guarantee optimal Attack and SPA ratings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trait Rolling and Transfer">
    Roll for high-tier traits like **Monarch** (0.1%) or **Ethereal** on standard evolved units first. Once achieved, use the **Shiny Transfer** mechanic to move that trait onto your Shiny variant, preserving your investment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Memorialia and Familia Integration">
    Equip unit-specific items. For example, Yamamoto requires the **Vanguard Familiar** from the Odyssey event (Rank 10) and the **Blazing Inferno** Memorialia to unlock his full skeleton-summoning potential.
  </Step>
</Steps>
