<Callout type="warning" title="The 32-Bit Integer Limit Bug">
If an enemy spawns with more than 4,294,967,295 HP (2^32 - 1), its health bar will appear visually bugged, making the enemy look invincible. This is a visual overflow issue that typically occurs in Sandbox Mode, late Infinite Mode waves, or during the Arin Boss Event. The enemy is still taking damage and can be defeated once its actual HP drops below this threshold.
</Callout>

When dealing with extreme HP scaling, players should continue targeting the enemy despite the static health bar. Utilizing percentage-based damage or stacking high-tier buffs will help bring the enemy's health down to readable levels.
