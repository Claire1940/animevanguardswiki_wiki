<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Section 3: Trait Synergies and Reroll Priorities">

Traits are permanent modifiers that can be rolled on your units to drastically improve their combat stats, such as Damage, Seconds Per Attack (SPA), and Range. Rerolling traits at the Mandra Traits NPC is a vital part of late-game min-maxing, but it can be highly resource-intensive. Understanding the pity thresholds and matching the right trait to the right unit role is crucial to avoid wasting your Trait Rerolls.

| Mythic Trait | Base Roll Chance | Pity Threshold | Key Benefit |
|--------------|------------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Solar** | 0.5% | 300 rolls | Balanced boost to both Damage and Range |
| **Deadeye** | 0.375% | 400 rolls | Crit-focused damage, ideal for boss killers |
| **Ethereal** | 0.175% | 858 rolls | Massive raw damage multiplier for main carries |
| **Monarch** | 0.1% | 1500 rolls | The ultimate trait; massive stats for low-placement units |

Not every unit needs a Mythic trait to be effective. Matching the trait's bonuses to what the unit actually does in your team composition will save you countless resources.

| Unit Role | Best Traits | Key Synergies & Reasons |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------------|
| **Single-Placement Carry** | Monarch, Ethereal, Deadeye | These units benefit the most from massive stat multipliers since you can only place one or two of them. |
| **Fast Attacking DPS** | Deadeye, Blitz, Ethereal | SPA and crit-focused traits scale incredibly well with units that already attack rapidly. |
| **Wide-Range AoE Unit** | Monarch, Solar, Marksman | Combining extra range with damage boosts allows these units to cover multiple lanes. |
| **Farm or Economy Unit** | Fortune, Swift | Economy units care about income timing and utility rather than raw offensive power. |
| **Early-game Filler** | Vigor, Range, Swift | Common traits are perfectly acceptable on temporary units that will eventually be replaced. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Pity System Warning">
The Trait Index menu tracks Mythic pity separately for each individual unit. Fortunately, major progression actions like **Evolution, Normalizing, and Stat Transfers do not reset your unit's trait pity**, allowing you to safely upgrade your characters without losing your progress.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Section 4: Evolution and Ascension Progression">

To unlock the true potential of the **anime vanguards best units**, you must guide them through the evolution and ascension systems. Evolution completely transforms a unit, granting them entirely new attack patterns, powerful passives, and higher base stats. Ascension, handled through the Kokashi NPC, provides incremental percentage-based stat boosts for your Mythic units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Long-Term Carry">
    Before farming materials, identify a unit that will remain a staple of your team across multiple game modes, such as Story, Legend, Raid, and Rift. Evolving temporary units is a common trap that wastes rare essence stones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Required Evolution Materials">
    Each unit has specific evolution requirements. Check the Evolve station in the Upgrades area to see what materials you need. Farm the corresponding Legend stages, Raids, or event shops to gather essence stones and gold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Evolution Quests">
    Some premium units, such as the Pirate-themed units introduced in Update 13.0, require you to complete specific Evolution Quests. These multi-step challenges must be fully cleared before the upgrade station will let you evolve the unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Rarity-Changing Evolution First">
    If a Mythic unit has an evolution path that upgrades its rarity to Secret or Vanguard, **always evolve the unit before ascending it**. Evolving a unit to a higher rarity wipes any active ascensions, meaning you will lose any duplicate copies invested beforehand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once your unit is in its final evolved form, you can begin the ascension process at the Kokashi NPC to unlock powerful stat multipliers.

| Ascension Level | Cost Requirements | Stat Bonus Granted |
|-----------------|-------------------|--------------------|
| **Ascension I** | Target unit + 1 duplicate | **Damage +3%** |
| **Ascension II** | Target unit + 1 duplicate + materials | **Range +5%** |
| **Ascension III** | Target unit + 2 duplicates + materials | **Damage +7%** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Memoria System Changes">
As of the Eternal Adversaries Update 13.5, Memoria progression has been streamlined. **Memoria evolutions have been removed**, and all Memoria are now evolved by default. Evolving a unit will automatically grant you their designated Memoria, and you can recycle duplicate Memoria into Memoria Scrap at the new recycling station.
</Callout>

</Section>
