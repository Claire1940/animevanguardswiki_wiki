<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Systems Changed by Updates">

Multiple core systems have changed to streamline progression. The most notable change in Update 13.5 is the rework of the Memoria system. Previously, players had to evolve Memoria manually, which required substantial grinding. Now, all Memoria are evolved by default, and evolving a unit automatically rewards you with its designated Memoria.

Duplicate Memoria can be broken down using the new Memoria Recycling mechanic to earn Memoria Scrap, which can be spent in the Exclusive Shop. Additionally, Memoria Leveling Tickets allow players to instantly boost a Memoria's level to 60.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Evolve Base Units First">
    Summon base Mythic, Secret, or Vanguard units. Evolve them using materials gathered from Legend Stages, Raids, or Event Shops. This process now automatically awards the unit's default evolved Memoria.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Memoria Inventory">
    Equip your newly acquired Memoria. Take any unwanted duplicates to the social hub, recycle them for Memoria Scrap, and trade the scrap for high-value progression items in the Exclusive Shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Unit Traits">
    Visit the Mandra Traits NPC to roll for high-tier traits like Monarch, Ethereal, or Deadeye. Evolution, normalization, and stat transfers do not reset your trait pity count.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Ascension Steps">
    For Mythic units that you plan to keep long-term, take unlocked, non-shiny duplicate copies to the Kokashi NPC to apply Ascension levels for permanent stat upgrades.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Ascension system remains a key endgame scaling mechanic. However, players must plan their upgrades carefully because evolving a Mythic unit into a Secret or Vanguard rarity form resets all active ascensions. Evolve the unit first, then apply ascension upgrades.

| Ascension Level | Stat Buff Type | Damage Bonus | Range Bonus | Copy Cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Ascension I | Base Damage | +3% Damage | None | 1 Duplicate |
| Ascension II | Attack Range | None | +5% Range | 1 Duplicate |
| Ascension III | Evolved Damage | +7% Damage | None | 1 Duplicate |

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Tip">
Use Memoria Leveling Tickets to quickly upgrade newly acquired Memoria. Bringing your equipped Memoria to Level 60 provides immediate stat scaling without forcing you to grind lower-tier stages.
</Callout>
