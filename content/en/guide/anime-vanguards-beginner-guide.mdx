<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering Traits, Rerolls, and Unit Evolution">

Traits are permanent passive modifiers that significantly boost a unit's performance. You can reroll these traits at the Mandra Traits NPC in the lobby. Additionally, evolving your units unlocks new skill trees, attack patterns, and massive stat multipliers.

The trait system features a built-in pity tracker that guarantees rare mythic traits after a set number of rolls. Always prioritize rerolling your primary carry units before optimizing support or economy units.

| Trait Name | Roll Chance | Role / Effect | Pity Threshold |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vigor** | 26.0% | Basic damage boost | None |
| **Solar** | 0.5% | High damage and range | 300 rolls |
| **Deadeye** | 0.375% | Crit-focused boss damage | 400 rolls |
| **Ethereal** | 0.175% | Top-tier meta carry damage | 858 rolls |
| **Monarch** | 0.1% | Highest stat boost (single placement) | 1500 rolls |

Evolution requires specific essence stones, gold, and sometimes unique quest completions (such as the Elastic Captain evolution route). In Update 13.5, unit evolutions also automatically grant their designated Memoria, removing the old requirement of evolving Memoria separately.

<Callout type="warning" title="Ascension Warning">
When a Mythic unit evolves into Secret or Vanguard rarity, its Ascensions are removed and do not transfer. Always evolve your unit to its final rarity before spending Ascension materials.
</Callout>
