<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Memoria System in Update 13.5">

The launch of the Eternal Adversaries Update 13.5 has completely overhauled the **anime vanguards memoria** system, streamlining how players upgrade their units and manage late-game progression. Memoria serves as an equipment-based modifier system that directly enhances unit performance through baseline damage boosts, range improvements, and cooldown reduction. 

In previous versions, players had to farm materials to manually evolve Memoria. With the recent Update 13.5 changes, Memoria evolution has been removed entirely. All Memoria are now obtained in their evolved forms by default. When you successfully evolve a unit, the game automatically grants the unit its designated Memoria.

| Feature | Pre-Update 13.5 System | Post-Update 13.5 System |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Evolution Status** | Required manual farming and evolution | Evolved by default upon acquisition |
| **Acquisition Route** | Standard banners and item crafting | Granted automatically via unit evolution |
| **Duplicates Handling** | Sat in inventory or wasted | Dismantled via Memoria Recycling |
| **Max Level Capacity** | Scaled slowly with standard food items | Instantly boostable to Level 60 with tickets |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Memoria Recycling and Leveling Tickets">

Update 13.5 introduced the Memoria Recycling feature to clean up player inventories and convert duplicate items into valuable progression materials. Unwanted Memoria can be dismantled at the recycling terminal inside the main social hub.

### Recycling Output Values

Dismantling a Memoria item yields Memoria Scrap. The volume and rarity of the returned scrap depend directly on the rarity of the dismantled item. This scrap can then be spent at the exclusive shop to buy Memoria Leveling Tickets, which instantly raise any equipped Memoria to Level 60.

| Dismantled Item Rarity | Scrap Type Returned | Leveling Ticket Value |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Mythic Memoria** | Standard Scrap | Low (requires multiple dints) |
| **Secret Memoria** | Rare Scrap | Medium (exchanges for 1-2 tickets) |
| **Vanguard Memoria** | Pristine Scrap | High (exchanges for 5+ tickets) |
