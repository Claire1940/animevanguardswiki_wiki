<Section icon={Swords} title="Passives and Combat Mechanics">

The true power of **anime vanguards yoruichi** lies within her dual passive abilities [https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)]. These passives completely redefine how she scales in the late game and allow her to synergize with support units in ways that standard physical or magic attackers cannot match [T1ydXAwL_so, https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)].

### Shunko Master Passive
Her first passive, **Shunko Master**, reduces her SPA by 5% with each consecutive attack, resetting on every third strike [https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)]. More importantly, it features a unique scaling mechanic: **all SPA buffs and debuffs applied to Yoruichi are also applied directly to her damage stat** [https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)]. This means that external speed boosts (such as those provided by support units like Brook) double-dip on her performance, giving her both faster attacks and higher raw damage simultaneously [T1ydXAwL_so, https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)].

### God of Lightning Passive
Unlocked upon evolving into her Raijin form, **God of Lightning** grants her complete immunity to status effects and debuffs [https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)]. Additionally, every attack she lands applies a **2-second Stun** [https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)]. If she attacks an enemy that is already stunned, she removes the stun but deals **50% more damage** and reduces her own SPA by **30% for 10 seconds** [https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)]. This creates an incredibly fast attack loop where she rapidly cycles through massive damage multipliers [T1ydXAwL_so, https://animevanguards.fandom.com/wiki/Units/Yuruicha_(Raijin)].
