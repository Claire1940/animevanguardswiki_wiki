<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Duo Transformation">

The unique appeal of **anime vanguards gohan** lies in its transformation mechanic. You start the match by placing Future Gohan, who functions as a high-efficiency damage dealer with unique placement rules. However, the unit's true potential is unlocked through the "Teacher's Anger" and "Avengers Arrival" passives, which bring Trunks onto the battlefield.

### The Two Phases of the Unit

Gohan utilizes the **Battlefield Education** passive. When you place your first non-farm unit, Gohan can essentially be placed for free or with significant discounts. Furthermore, placing other units near him provides instant upgrades to both Gohan and the new unit. His attack pattern evolves from a single-handed Masenko to a full AoE Special Beam Cannon as he reaches higher upgrade tiers.

| Phase | Form Name | Primary Attack | AoE Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | Future Gohan | One-handed Masenko | Cone / Circular |
| **Phase 2** | Enraged Trunks | Avengers Arrival | Massive Circular |
| **Trigger** | Initial Placement | 0 Life Stocks / Revive | N/A |

### Trunks: The Enraged Powerhouse

Trunks only appears when the player's life stocks reach zero or a revive ability (like those from Estarossa or specific relics) is triggered. When Trunks arrives, he is automatically at **Max Upgrade**. He repulses all enemies on the map and gains a 100% critical chance bonus. This makes him one of the most reliable "clutch" units in the 2026 meta.

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Death">

</Callout>

### Memoria: Bond of Master and Disciple

The **Bond of Master and Disciple** Memoria is tailor-made for this unit. It increases critical damage by 30% and provides a "cleanse" effect on critical hits. Since Trunks has a built-in 100% crit chance upon arrival, this Memoria ensures every single hit is a devastating blow that also keeps the unit free from debuffs.

| Stat Priority | Target Rating | Effect on Gohan/Trunks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical Damage** | **Godly / Z+** | Multiplies Trunks' 100% crit rate |
| **Attack** | **Z+** | Increases base scaling for both forms |
| **Range** | **Z** | Ensures full map coverage for AoE |
