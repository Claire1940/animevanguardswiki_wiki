<Section icon={Zap} title="Obtainment and Evolution Process">

To play as the **anime vanguards cid** Lord of Shadows, you must master the transition from his "John Smith" persona. This process is unique to this unit and requires tactical awareness during the heat of battle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deploy Smith John">
    Place the Smith John unit on the map. Ensure he is in a position where he can consistently hit enemies, as his meter only fills during active combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Charge the Swap Meter">
    Every attack Smith John performs refills the Swap Meter by **13%**. You will need approximately 8 successful attacks to reach the 100% threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate 'It was me!'">
    Once the meter hits 100%, a special action button labeled **"It was me!"** will appear. Clicking this instantly transforms the unit into the Lord of Shadows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Form Swaps">
    You can swap back and forth between forms. However, remember that the Lord of Shadows is the primary DPS form, while Smith John serves as the gateway and utility provider.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stack Reset Warning">
The **Dramatic Entry** buff (Damage and Range) is lost immediately upon swapping. Only swap if you intend to use the **All Range Atomic** ability or if you need the specific attack pattern of the other form.
</Callout>

</Section>
