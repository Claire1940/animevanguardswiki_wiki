<Section icon={Swords} title="Absolute Zero Mechanics and Element Interactions">

The core strength of **anime vanguards rukia** lies in her unique "Absolute Zero" status effect. When Rukia attacks, she inflicts Freeze for 2 seconds and applies Absolute Zero. This status is a reactive debuff that changes its behavior depending on the element of the ally who hits the enemy next. This makes her the most versatile support unit in the current meta.

| Element | Interaction Effect | Strategic Value |
|---------|--------------------|-----------------|
| **Fire** | +100% DoT Damage (Excludes Intense Burn) | Extreme synergy with Esganor |
| **Water** | +50% Damage and 50% Slow for 20s | Best for crowd control and stalling |
| **Spark** | Attack damage repeats | Massive burst potential for single-hit units |
| **Holy** | +20% Damage buff to allies for 20s | Best for overall team DPS scaling |
| **Nature** | Extends Freeze to 4 seconds | Essential for stopping fast bosses |
| **Cosmic** | +30% Damage and reapplies Absolute Zero | Sustained debuff looping |

In addition to these interactions, Rukia possesses the **Frozen Cancellation** passive. When she freezes an enemy for the first time, she inflicts Nullify. Attacking a Nullified enemy grants her a flat 50% damage bonus. Furthermore, her total damage scales by 100% multiplied by her current available placement limit, making her a powerhouse in modes where multiple placements are allowed.

One of the most revolutionary aspects of **anime vanguards rukia** is her ability to function without being placed on the map. This "Unyielding Support" mechanic triggers whenever an allied unit attacks, causing Rukia to briefly appear and perform a follow-up strike.

- **Status Application**: She still applies Freeze and Absolute Zero during these follow-up attacks.
