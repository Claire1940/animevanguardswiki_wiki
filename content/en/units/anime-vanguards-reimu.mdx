<Section icon={Swords} title="Evolution Path and Ascension">

Evolving Reimu is not just a stat boost; it is a fundamental shift in her utility. In Update 13.5, the evolution process has been streamlined, but it still requires significant material farming. You will need to visit the Upgrades area and ensure you have completed any necessary Evolution Quests.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Material Gathering">
    Collect the required Essence Stones (typically Rainbow and Green) and unit-specific materials. For Reimu, these are often found in the **Legend Stages** or the **Event Shop**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Evolution Quest Completion">
    Some versions of Reimu require specific "Spirit Tasks" or kill counts. Ensure she is in your active team during your farm runs to track progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Upgrade Station">
    Head to the Upgrades area. Ensure the unit is unlocked and not "Shiny" (unless you are evolving a Shiny version). Pay the Gold cost to finalize the evolution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ascension Planning">
    Once evolved, use duplicate copies at the Kokashi NPC to Ascend. Ascension I provides a **3% Damage boost**, which is vital for the current Update 13.5 scaling.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Map} title="Team Synergy and Game Mode Strategy">

Reimu is a team player. While she can solo early Story stages, her true potential is unlocked when paired with economy units and heavy hitters. In the **Eternal Adversaries** update, team composition is more important than ever due to the new "Dispersal" enemy mechanics.

### Recommended Team Compositions

| Unit Pair | Synergy Reason | Mode Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Reimu + Demon Leader** | Cost reduction and massive team ceiling. | Raids / Boss Rush |
| **Reimu + God (Heaven)** | Time stop allows Reimu to stack damage. | Infinite / Worldlines |
| **Reimu + Today's Strongest** | Dual-core DPS setup for Update 13.5. | Summer Portals |
| **Reimu + Ice Queen** | Perma-freeze and AoE shred combo. | Legend Stages |

### Mode-Specific Tactics
- **Story Mode**: Focus on early placement. Reimu's base damage is high enough to carry through Wave 15 without heavy upgrades.
- **Raids**: Use Reimu for "Add Control." Let your single-target units focus on the boss while Reimu clears the smaller, faster minions that can leak.
- **Summer Gamemode**: Prioritize SPA (Blitz trait). The zombie-style waves require fast, repeated hits to prevent the lane from being overwhelmed.
