<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Upgrade Path and Stat Scaling">

As you upgrade Lord Friezo (Emperor) during a match, his attack patterns evolve significantly. He starts with "The Emperor's Army" (a cone attack) and eventually transitions into "Murder Ball," a massive 22-range circle AoE that can wipe out entire waves.

<Callout type="info" title="Golden Transformation">
At Upgrade 4, Frieza transforms into his Golden form. This is purely visual for the standard model, but it signals a massive jump in his damage-per-second (DPS) capabilities.
</Callout>

**Key Attack Patterns:**
- **The Emperor's Army**: A 60-degree cone attack with 5 hits. Great for narrow corridors.
- **Murder Rain**: A full AoE attack with 3 hits, unlocked at Upgrade 4.
- **Murder Barrage**: A wide line attack (10 range wide) with 5 hits, excellent for long straightaways.
- **Murder Ball**: His ultimate attack. A huge circular AoE with 5 hits and a 22-unit radius.
