<Section icon={Swords} title="Skill Breakdown and Damage Scaling">

Understanding the kit of **anime vanguards brook** is essential for high-wave survival in 2026. His kit is divided into a passive buffing state and an active "nuke" ability. The synergy between these two makes him a staple for any serious team composition.

The "King of String" passive is his bread and butter. Upon activation, a rhythm game appears on the screen. By hitting notes accurately, players increase the damage buff for all allies in Brook's range. Starting at a base 20% buff, each successful note adds 1%, capping at a massive 60% total damage increase. 

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Failing the rhythm game or missing too many notes will result in a significantly lower buff. If you are playing on a high-latency connection, practice the timing in lower-difficulty maps first.
</Callout>

His second major skill, "Hellis Frozen," is what makes him a viable DPS even in late-game scenarios. It deals 300% of the highest damage stat currently present on the field. This means if your strongest unit is hitting for 1,000,000 damage, Brook's ultimate will strike for 3,000,000 in a forward line while freezing enemies for 2 seconds.

| Skill Name | Effect Description | Scaling/Cap |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **King of String** | Buffs allies in range by 20% base damage | Max 60% (1% per note) |
| **Hellis Frozen** | Deals massive line damage and freezes enemies | 300% of highest field DMG |
| **Melody Retention** | Buff persists after the active skill ends | 300 Second Duration |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate King of String">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Note Synchronization">
    Watch the falling notes on the screen. Press the corresponding keys (or tap the screen on mobile) as the notes pass through the hit zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain the Combo">
    Each successful hit increases the buff percentage by 1%. Aim for at least 40 hits to surpass the standard buffing units in the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Hellis Frozen">
    Wait for a dense wave of enemies to align in a straight path before triggering the freeze and damage nuke.
  </Step>
</Steps>
