<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Traits and Rerolls for Shinobi Units">

Traits are permanent modifiers that drastically alter a unit's combat efficiency. In Anime Vanguards, traits are rolled using Trait Rerolls at the Mandra Traits NPC. For Shinobi-class units (including Narutu, Cursed Shinobi, and Fastest Shinobi), selecting a trait that complements their high attack speed or damage over time is vital to maximizing their performance.

However, you must exercise caution when spending these rare items. Trait Rerolls should be preserved for units that will remain in your active roster for the long term.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Preservation Warning">
Never spend Trait Rerolls on your base Narutu starter unit. Because starters are replaced early in your progression, spending rerolls on them will severely set back your endgame preparation. Save all rerolls for your first Mythic, Secret, or Vanguard units.
</Callout>
