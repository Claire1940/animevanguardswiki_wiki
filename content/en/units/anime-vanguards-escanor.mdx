<Section icon={Target} title="Evolution & Ascension Guide">

Maximizing the power of your **anime vanguards escanor** unit requires a long-term progression plan involving both Evolution and Ascension. Evolution changes the unit's base form, unlocking superior skills, altered attack patterns, and higher upgrade caps. Ascension, handled through the Kokashi NPC, provides incremental stat upgrades using duplicate copies of the unit.

Following a structured upgrade path ensures you do not waste rare materials or accidentally reset valuable progression milestones.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Base Unit">
    Obtain Iscanur (Pride) from the standard summon banner rotation using Gems. Ensure you secure at least one copy for your main team and hold onto any duplicates for later ascension phases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Evolution Materials">
    Farm the designated stages, raids, or event shops to collect the necessary essence stones, gold, and unit-specific evolution items. Avoid partial investments in other units until Escanor's full material sheet is complete.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Evolution Quests">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Ascension Steps">
    Once evolved, visit the Kokashi NPC to begin Ascension. Feed duplicate, non-evolved, unlocked copies of the unit to unlock permanent multi-level stat bonuses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Ascension offers three distinct tiers of power, which are highly recommended if you plan to use Iscanur (Pride) as your primary lane carry in late-game challenges:
