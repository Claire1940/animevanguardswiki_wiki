<Section icon={Target} title="Passive Abilities and Combat Synergy">

Beyond her forms, **anime vanguards erza** possesses some of the most complex passives in the game. Understanding the interaction between her meter and her buffs is key to maximizing her efficiency.

**Team Leader Passive:**
Whenever Erza moves locations (using her Valkyrie teleport or manual placement), she gains a **50% Damage Buff** for 30 seconds. Furthermore, if she is currently being buffed by another unit, her attacks **bypass all enemy damage reduction**. This makes her the ultimate boss killer when paired with other supports.

**Burning Heart Passive:**
Spending meter to switch forms inflicts **Bleed** on all enemies in range (10% damage for 8 seconds). If there is already an enemy with a Damage-over-Time (DOT) effect in her range, she generates **double meter** on every attack. This creates a loop where she can swap forms almost constantly if your team is applying Burn or Poison.

| Passive Name | Trigger Condition | Effect Granted |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Team Leader** | Unit Movement | +50% Damage for 30s |
| **Re-quip Meter** | Attacking | 15% Meter gain (30% if DOT present) |
| **Burning Heart** | Swapping Armor | AoE Bleed (10% Dmg) |
| **Buff Infliction** | Losing a Buff | Targeted enemy receives Bleed |

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Insight">
The "Passion" element in her Vanguard form is currently meta-defining. It doesn't just buff damage; it reduces the cost of other units. This allows for faster scaling in the early waves of Infinite Mode.
