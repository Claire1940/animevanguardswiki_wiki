<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Corruption Mechanics and Tier Priority">

Corruption is the defining feature of the **anime vanguards chopper**. By using Corruption Crystals collected from the Odyssey realm, you can tap into four distinct levels of power. Each level provides a different utility, but not all are created equal for the current meta.

| Corruption Level | Effect Name | Mechanic Description | Meta Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Corruption I** | Nullify | Applies Nullify status on all attacks | Situational |
| **Corruption II** | Rupture | Applies Rupture status on all attacks | Low |
| **Corruption III** | Efficiency | Decreases unit placement/upgrade price by 10% | Medium |
| **Corruption IV** | Shield Shred | Grants **5 extra hits** to Shielded enemies | **Essential** |

Corruption IV is widely considered the "God Roll" for The Doctor. Many top-tier units, such as Escanor, struggle with multi-hit capabilities. By equipping a Corruption IV Chopper, these units can melt through enemies with 20+ shields in seconds, effectively removing their only major weakness.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
Corruption is a permanent status. While you can use items to "Reverse" the stat rolls of a Corruption III familiar, you cannot remove the corruption status once it is applied. Choose your base familiar wisely before committing crystals.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Farming Guide: HAPPY Factory Raid">

To obtain the **anime vanguards chopper**, you must participate in the HAPPY Factory Raid. There are two primary acts, each offering vastly different drop rates and difficulty levels. While Act 1 is accessible for lower-level players, Act 2 is the gold standard for serious farmers.

| Raid Act | Difficulty | Chopper Drop Rate | Secondary Drops |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Act 1** | Normal | 2% Chance | Gold, Basic Items |
| **Act 2** | Hard | 50% Chance | Shiny Doflamingo (Rare) |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Code Phase">
    In Act 1, focus on finding the door codes. If you find at least one or two numbers, you can often guess the remaining digits to save time. Speed is key to maximizing runs per hour.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Protection Phase">
    In Act 2, the primary challenge is protecting innocent civilians. Units like Obito are excellent here because they can clear enemies without accidentally hitting the civilians. If a civilian is hit, the run is often an instant failure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Barrel RNG">
    During the barrel phase, positioning is everything. Ensure your high-DPS units are placed to intercept barrels from multiple lanes. Good barrel RNG can significantly shorten the boss encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Boss Execution">
    Once Doflamingo appears, use buffing units like Nami or Takaroda to amplify your main DPS. If you are using Escanor, ensure his burn damage is active to maximize the Shred effect from Chopper.
  </Step>
</Steps>
