<Section icon={Target} title="Obtaining and Evolving Saiyan Units">

Securing these top-tier units requires a combination of Gem management and understanding the banner rotation. Most Goku-inspired units are found within the high-rarity pools of the Summon Banner, particularly during special event windows. Once obtained, the real progression begins with the Evolution and Ascension systems, which significantly boost a unit's base stats and unlock new passive abilities that are vital for 2026's difficult Raid content.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summoning Strategy">
    Save your Gems for the Seasonal or Targeted banners where Rogita or Brolzi are featured. The pity system ensures a Secret or Vanguard unit after a set number of pulls, so avoid "rage-pulling" on standard banners without sufficient resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gathering Materials">
    Evolution requires specific items such as Essence Stones and unit-specific materials. For Saiyan units, you will often need to farm Legend Stages or Raid modes to collect the necessary components for their "Super" or "Ascended" forms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Executing Evolution">
    Navigate to the Upgrades area and use the Evolve station. Ensure your unit is unlocked and you have the required Gold. Evolution often changes the unit's attack pattern, such as shifting from a single-target strike to a massive AoE beam.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ascension Planning">
    For Mythic and higher units, use the Kokashi NPC for Ascension. This provides incremental Damage and Range boosts. Note that Ascension I provides a 3% damage boost, while reaching Ascension III grants a substantial 7% damage increase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Evolution Stage | Material 1 | Material 2 | Gold Cost |

| **Evolved to Vanguard** | 100 Essence Stones | 10 Raid Drops | 75,000 |
