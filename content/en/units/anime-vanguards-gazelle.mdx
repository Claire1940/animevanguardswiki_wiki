<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution Guide: Transforming into Gazelle (Zombie)">

Evolving your **anime vanguards gazelle** into her **Zombie** form significantly boosts her damage output and unlocks the full potential of her passive abilities. The evolution process is resource-intensive, requiring a mix of specific event items and various essences that can be crafted or found in challenges.

### Evolution Requirements

Before you can trigger the evolution, you must reach **5,000 takedowns** with Gazelle. This is best achieved by placing her in Infinite Mode on lower-difficulty maps like Sand Village or Planet Namic, where enemies are weak and plentiful.

| Item Name | Quantity Required | Primary Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Energy Bone** | 1 | Love Portal Drop (5% Chance) |
| **Green Essence** | 30 | Challenges / Crafting |
| **Blue Essence** | 20 | Challenges / Crafting |
| **Purple Essence** | 15 | Legend Stages / Crafting |
| **Yellow Essence** | 12 | Challenges / Crafting |
| **Rainbow Essence** | 2 | High-tier Crafting / Shops |
| **Gold** | 15,000 | Story Mode / Infinite Mode |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Farm the Energy Bone">
    Enter Love Portals (preferably Tier 5 or higher). The Energy Bone has a low drop rate, so multiple runs are usually required. It is a Mythic-tier evolution item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete Takedown Milestone">
    Equip Gazelle and enter an Infinite Mode match. You need exactly 5,000 kills. Focus on early-game waves to rack up numbers quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Essences">
    Use the Crafting station to convert lower-tier essences into the required Purple and Rainbow variants. You will need a significant amount of Blue and Green base materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Evolution">
    Navigate to the Evolve menu in the lobby, select Gazelle, and consume the materials and 15,000 Gold to unlock **Gazelle (Zombie)**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="Passive Abilities: Corpse Party & Pure Evil">

The true strength of the **anime vanguards gazelle** unit lies in her two unique passives. These abilities transform the battlefield by turning the enemy's numbers against them.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Corpse Party!">
    Every 5 attacks, Gazelle revives all enemies killed by any unit during that timeframe as allies. These zombies deal **30% of Gazelle's damage** and can stack up to **120 total zombies** per placement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pure Evil">
    For every zombie ally currently on the field, Gazelle receives a **1% damage buff**. This buff can scale up to a maximum of **50% bonus damage**, rewarding high-density waves.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

### Strategic Application
To maximize the "Pure Evil" buff, Gazelle should be placed where other high-DPS units (like Song Jinwu or Gujo) are already thinning the herd. Since the zombies are revived from *any* unit's kills, placing Gazelle mid-track allows her to pick up the "corpses" left behind by your primary damage dealers.

**Optimal Trait Rankings:**
1. **Monarch**: The absolute best for stats and scaling.
2. **Solar**: Excellent for consistency across different map types.
3. **Deadeye**: Necessary if you find her base range too restrictive for certain maps.

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Synergy">
Pair Gazelle with units that have high SBA (Attack Speed) to ensure a constant stream of kills for her to revive. The more zombies on the field, the higher her personal damage becomes.
</Callout>
