<Section icon={Swords} title="Abilities and Passive Mechanics">

The strength of **anime vanguards benimaru** lies in his complex interaction with status effects. He is not just a raw damage dealer; he is a specialist in area-of-effect (AOE) burn management. His kit introduces the "Conflagration" mechanic, which turns standard burn ticks into explosive AOE damage that spreads to nearby enemies.

| Passive Name | Effect Description | Key Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Master of the Broom** | Generates up to 5 brooms; selling fires them for 80% DMG. | High burst potential. |
| **Best of Both Worlds** | Applies Burn (50% DMG); inflicts Conflagration on burnt foes. | Continuous DOT scaling. |
| **Master of Flames** | Damages enemies with Conflagration to inflict Nullify. | Disables enemy abilities. |
| **Scorched** | Advanced burn state triggered by broom impacts. | Massive AOE damage. |

When **anime vanguards benimaru** attacks an enemy already suffering from a burn effect, he upgrades that status to Conflagration. This state persists as long as the initial burn is active. The most critical part of this mechanic is that every time a burn tick occurs on a Conflagration-afflicted target, it deals damage to up to three additional enemies in a small radius.
