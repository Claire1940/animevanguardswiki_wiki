<Section icon={Swords} title="Anime Vanguards Whitebeard Unit Overview">

In the competitive landscape of Anime Vanguards, **Edward** (familiarly known as Whitebeard) stands out as one of the most reliable ground-based damage dealers. As a Mythic-rarity unit, Edward possesses an incredibly high damage ceiling, which scales dramatically once evolved into his final form, Edward (Unleashed). 

His core kit revolves around massive shockwaves that deal heavy area-of-effect (AoE) damage to entire waves of enemies. While his initial placement cost is moderately high, his scaling efficiency makes him a staple choice for clearing high-difficulty content, including Raids, Legend Stages, and Boss Rush modes.

<Callout type="info" title="Cost Management Tip">
Edward's early-game placement cost is moderately high, making him less suitable as a first-wave starter but exceptionally strong as a mid-game transition carry once your economy units are established.

During a match, Edward transitions through several upgrade phases. His early upgrades focus on expanding his attack range, while his late-stage upgrades drastically boost his attack speed (SPA) and unlock his signature Tremor shockwaves. This makes him highly adaptable to both short corridors and wide-open maps.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Evolve Whitebeard in Anime Vanguards">

Evolving Edward into his Unleashed form is essential for unlocking his full damage potential and his advanced shockwave attack patterns. The evolution process requires a mix of specific crafting materials, elemental essences, and a gold investment.

| Material Type | Quantity Required | Primary Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tremor Orb** | 1 | Crafted via Lobby NPC / Raid Drops |

| **Rainbow Essence** | 5 | Crafting / Special Event Shops |

To complete the evolution process successfully, follow these step-by-step instructions at the upgrade area in the main lobby.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summon Base Unit">
    Obtain the base Edward unit from the standard summon banners using your accumulated Gems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Essence Stones">
    Farm the required Red, Green, and Rainbow Essence Stones by completing daily challenges and clearing high-tier Legend Stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft the Tremor Orb">
    Visit the crafting NPC in the lobby to assemble the Tremor Orb using your gathered essences and base materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Evolution">
    Navigate to the Evolve station, select your unlocked Edward unit, ensure all materials are present, and confirm the evolution to unlock Edward (Unleashed).
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Callout>

Once evolved, Edward (Unleashed) gains a massive boost to his base attack stats, and his final upgrade unlocks a global-scale tremor attack that can slow down and damage enemies across the entire map path.
