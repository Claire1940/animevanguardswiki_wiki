<Section icon={Swords} title="Monarch Armin Abilities & Buff Mechanics">

Monarch Armin operates on a unique set of mechanics that distinguish him from traditional support units. Instead of buffing units within a standard range circle, he creates specific placement zones on the track. Understanding how these zones interact with your team is crucial for maximizing your defensive setup.

### Placement Buff Zones
Every time you place Monarch Armin or upgrade him, he designates a placement location near the track within his range. Any unit placed inside this designated circle receives two major benefits:
- **Upgrade Cost Reduction**: Reduces the upgrade cost of the placed unit by **10%**.
- **Damage Buff**: Increases the placed unit's damage output by **20%**.

Once a unit is placed in the designated zone, the circle is consumed. If you activate Monarch Armin's active ability, any unused placement zones are removed from the map.

### Nuclear Giant Transformation
His active ability, **Nuclear Giant**, transforms the player into a Colossal Titan. This ability has a **600-second cooldown** and operates under the following rules:
- **Damage Scaling**: The Titan takes on the highest damage stat of any unit currently within Monarch Armin's range.
- **Map-wide Damage**: Every step the Titan takes deals **60%** of that highest damage stat to all enemies on the map.
- **Debuff Immunity**: The unit whose damage is copied becomes completely immune to status effects and debuffs for the duration of the active ability.
