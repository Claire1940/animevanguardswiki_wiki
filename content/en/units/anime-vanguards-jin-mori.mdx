<Section icon={Swords} title="Passive Skills & Combat Mechanics">

Jin Mori's viability in the endgame meta is driven by three incredibly synergistic passive abilities [1]. These skills allow him to ignore enemy defenses, scale his own damage dynamically, and act as a universal wildcard for team synergy buffs [1].

| Passive Ability | In-Game Effect | Meta Application |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **The Power of All Races** | Counts as part of every group/category. Gains a 10% damage buff for each different group on your team [1]. | Bypasses restrictive stage rules and maximizes team synergy buffs automatically [1]. |
| **Monkey Clones** | Grants +50% damage and +5% range for every clone created. Selling the original unit transfers all buffs to clones for 342 seconds [1]. | Allows for an explosive late-game DPS burst by selling the main unit during boss waves [1]. |
| **Soul Strike** | Ignores 30% of enemy damage reduction. Amplifies output damage by the enemy's damage reduction percentage [1]. | Essential for shredding high-defense bosses in late-game Worldlines and raids [1]. |

### The Power of All Races
This passive places Jin Mori into the "Every Group" category [1]. In Anime Vanguards, many maps and stages apply specific buffs or restrictions based on unit groups (such as Saiyans, Soul Reapers, or Pirates) [1]. Jin Mori automatically qualifies for all of them [1]. Furthermore, by diversifying your team composition with different categories, you can easily secure a permanent 40% damage buff for him [1].

### Soul Strike
As enemies scale into higher waves, their damage reduction (DR) percentages skyrocket. Soul Strike directly counters this by ignoring 30% of their DR [1]. Even better, it turns the enemy's defense into a weapon: it amplifies Jin Mori's damage by the exact percentage of the enemy's remaining damage reduction, ensuring his DPS remains consistent against heavily armored targets [1].
