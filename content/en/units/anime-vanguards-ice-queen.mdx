<Section icon={Target} title="The Unyielding Support Mechanic">

The defining feature of the **anime vanguards ice queen** is her **Unyielding Support** passive. This ability allows her to appear as a phantom attacker whenever an allied unit strikes an enemy. This attack does not count toward your unit placement limit, effectively giving you a "free" extra unit on the field at all times.

Beyond just damage, this follow-up attack applies two massive buffs to the assisted unit: a 50% damage increase and a 20-point range increase for 20 seconds. This makes her mandatory for maximizing the effectiveness of other meta units like Broly or Guts.
