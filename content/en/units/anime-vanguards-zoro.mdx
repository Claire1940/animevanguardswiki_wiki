<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Evolve Zoro to Bounty Hunter (King of Hell)">

Evolving Zoro requires completing specific challenges and gathering rare resources. The transformation to **Bounty Hunter (King of Hell)** dramatically changes his moveset, adding massive linear area-of-effect slashes that can hit dozens of enemies in a single strike.

To complete the evolution process, players must unlock the Evolve station in the Upgrades area. The requirements involve a combination of standard essence stones, gold, and event-exclusive materials.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base Bounty Hunter">
    Ensure you have unlocked the base Bounty Hunter unit through summons or Rift rewards. Keep the unit unlocked and remove it from any active teams before attempting evolution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Pirate Dynasty and Rifts">
    Participate in the Pirate Dynasty game mode and specific Rift stages. These areas drop the required upgrade materials and provide progress toward Evolution Quests.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Upgrade Materials">
    Collect the necessary amount of Green Essence, Rainbow Essence, and gold. The evolved form demands a significant material investment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Evolution at Upgrades NPC">
    Bring the base unit, materials, and required gold to the Evolve station. Confirm the process to receive Bounty Hunter (King of Hell).
  </Step>
</Steps>
