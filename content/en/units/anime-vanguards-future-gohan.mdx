The Gohan and Trunks duo unit operates on a highly unique dual-form system that transitions based on your in-game life stocks [1]. When initially deployed, you control Gohan, who functions as an exceptional early-to-mid-game defender [1]. Once your life stocks are depleted to zero, Gohan is replaced by Enraged Trunks, who immediately deploys at maximum upgrade level and repulses all enemies on the map [1].

Gohan's primary passives revolve around Battlefield Education and Teacher's Anger [1]. Battlefield Education allows Gohan to deploy for free the moment you place your first non-farm unit [1]. Additionally, this passive instantly upgrades both Gohan and the placed unit by two levels, saving you a massive amount of early-game cash [1]. Teacher's Anger increases Gohan's critical chance whenever an ally is sold or a life stock is removed, scaling his damage directly with his critical hit rate [1].
