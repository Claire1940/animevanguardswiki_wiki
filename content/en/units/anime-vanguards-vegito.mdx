<Section icon={Map} title="How to Find Potara Earrings & Evolve Roku">

Before you can fuse into **anime vanguards vegito**, you must first evolve your base Roku unit into Roku (Super 3) [12]. This process requires a massive grind of 5,000 takedowns and two hidden evolution items: the Left and Right Potara Earrings. These items do not drop automatically; they spawn physically on the map with a low probability, requiring manual collection.

| Evolution Item | Mode Requirement | Spawn Location Details | Spawn Chance | Collection Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Left Potara Earring | Story Mode (Buu Saga) | Near the blast crater on the left path | 5% at match start | Walk up and press 'E' |
| Right Potara Earring | Legend Stage | On top of the tall mountain near enemy spawn | 5% at match start | Walk up and press 'E' |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Farm 5,000 Takedowns">
    Equip your base Roku unit and head into Infinite Mode (Namek is highly recommended due to high enemy density). Farm until you reach exactly 5,000 kills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hunt the Left Potara Earring">
    Load into the final Buu Saga Story Mode stage. At the start of the match, check if the system notification announces a Potara has spawned. If not, restart immediately to roll the 5% chance again. Locate the tiny earring near the blast crater and interact with it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hunt the Right Potara Earring">
    Load into the Legend Stage. Repeat the restart method until the notification appears. Scan the top of the mountains near the path. The earring is incredibly small, so zoom in and look for a tiny yellow glimmer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evolve to Roku (Super 3)">
    Navigate to the Evolve menu in the lobby, select Roku, consume the two Potara Earrings along with the required gold, and evolve him into Roku (Super 3) [12].
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Dual-Swap Gauge & Super Soul Bomb">

Roku (Super 3) functions via a dynamic **Swap Mechanic** [12]. When placed on the field, the active unit builds a swap gauge. Every basic attack fills the gauge by 25%. Once the gauge reaches 100%, you can manually swap between Roku (Super 3) and Vogita (Angel), or enable the Auto-Swap configuration to cycle through them automatically [12].

| Active Form | Swap Buff (40s Duration) | On-Entry Utility Effect | Unique Special Ability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Roku (Super 3)** | +50% Damage | Stuns all enemies in radius for 2 seconds | Super Soul Bomb (Mapwide Nuke) |
| **Vogita (Angel)** | -30% SPA | Slows all enemies in radius by 40% for 10s | Potara Fusion (Requires 13 Swaps) [12] |

The Super Soul Bomb is a game-changing ability. When activated while Roku is active, he retreats to the background to charge a massive Spirit Bomb for 3 minutes. During this time, Vogita (Angel) must hold the line alone. Once fully charged, it unleashes a devastating mapwide blast dealing 1,000% of the unit's base damage stat before swapping Roku back into active play.

<Callout type="tip" title="Swap Optimization">
Keep Auto-Swap enabled during regular waves to constantly refresh the 40-second damage and SPA buffs. Disable Auto-Swap only when you need to freeze a boss with Roku's entry stun or prepare for the Super Soul Bomb nuke.
</Callout>
