<Section icon={Target} title="Best Traits and Stat Optimization">

Traits are permanent modifiers that can significantly alter the performance of your **anime vanguards luffy**. Because this unit scales heavily into the late game, players should be selective with their **Trait Rerolls**. Using rare resources on a non-evolved version is generally considered a waste of materials.

| Trait Name | Rarity | Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monarch** | Mythic | +Damage, +Range, -Placement | **Cog 5th** Form |
| **Ethereal** | Mythic | Massive Damage Boost | Main Carry Role |
| **Deadeye** | Mythic | Critical Hit Scaling | Boss Killing |
| **Blitz** | Legendary | +Attack Speed (SPA) | Wave Clearing |
| **Fortune** | Rare | +Gold Earned | Early Story Farming |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Do not use your **Trait Rerolls** until you have reached **Level 30** and unlocked the higher-tier pity systems. Pity for Mythic traits does not reset during evolution, so you can safely hunt for **Monarch** on your Pirate form.
</Callout>

In addition to traits, you should use **Stat Chips** at the Kokashi NPC to optimize his base rolls. Aim for at least an **S-rank** in Damage and Range. Since Luffy's attacks have a wide swing, increasing his range allows him to cover multiple lanes simultaneously, which is essential for **Raid** maps.

</Section>
