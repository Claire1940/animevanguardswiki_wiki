<Section icon={Target} title="Passive Abilities and Explosive Mechanics">

What sets Reze apart from other AoE units is her dual-passive system. She doesn't just hit hard; she sets up chain reactions that can clear entire waves of high-health enemies. Understanding how "Detonation" and "Hidden Spy" interact is key to mastering her gameplay.

**Detonation Passive**  
Whenever Reze attacks an enemy, they become "Primed." If she (or another copy of Reze) attacks a Primed enemy, it triggers an explosion dealing 100% bonus damage. This explosion is not limited to the single target; it hits up to three additional nearby enemies. This makes her exceptionally strong against "clumped" waves where enemies are stacked on top of each other.

**Hidden Spy Passive**  
Every fifth wave, Reze triggers a unique mechanic where she gains a 50% damage buff for 30 seconds if certain conditions are met during the wave. This "Hidden Spy" buff is what allows her to jump from her standard 1.5 million damage to over 2.4 million damage mid-match.
