<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and I Sacrifice Ability">

The defining feature of the evolved **anime vanguards griffith** is his active ability, **I, Sacrifice**. This ability represents his transformation and the cost of his ambition, translating into a powerful map-wide debuff and buff system. Understanding the timing of this ability is the difference between a successful raid and a defeat.

When activated, a symbol of sacrifice appears over Griffith's head and shoots across the entire map. It marks every tower you own and every non-boss enemy currently on the path.

| Ability Stat | Value | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Type** | Active | Requires manual activation by the player. |
| **Cooldown** | 300 Seconds | A long local cooldown; use sparingly. |
| **Duration** | 3 Waves | The effect persists through multiple rounds. |
| **Range** | Global | Affects the entire map regardless of placement. |

**Strategic Ability Usage:**
- **Wave Management**: Save the ability for the final waves of a stage where enemy density is highest.
- **Boss Prep**: Since it marks non-boss enemies, use it right before a boss spawns to clear the "trash" mobs quickly, allowing your towers to focus on the boss.
- **Tower Synergy**: The marking of self-owned towers often interacts with specific traits or other unit buffs, increasing overall team efficiency.

<Callout type="info" title="Cooldown Management">
The 300-second cooldown is "Local," meaning it only applies to that specific Griffith unit. If you have multiple (though placement is usually limited to 1), they do not share the timer.
</Callout>
