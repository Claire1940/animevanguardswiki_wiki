<Section icon={Swords} title="Passive Abilities and Skill Mechanics">

The effectiveness of **anime vanguards orihime** is defined by two distinct passives that trigger at different stages of the match. Understanding these is the difference between a wasted slot and a tactical advantage.

### You Will Not Die! (Primary Support)
This is her active support mechanic. When placed, Orehimi attaches a hairpin to an ally. This provides a **15% damage increase** and, crucially, **cleanses any Status Effect or debuff**. This makes her exceptionally useful in stages where enemies apply stuns or burn effects to your towers.

### ICHIGA!!! (Economic Passive)
This passive is a game-changer for early-game setups. The first time you place a unit that costs **2,000 Yen or lower**, the cost is reduced to zero. 

| Passive Name | Effect Description | Limit/Cooldown |
|--------------|--------------------|----------------|
| **You Will Not Die!** | 15% Dmg Buff + Cleanse | 20s Local Cooldown |
| **ICHIGA!!!** | First unit <= 2000 is free | Once per match |

<Callout type="warning" title="Passive Limitation">
The "ICHIGA!!!" passive only works for the **very first** eligible unit placed after Orehimi. You cannot sell and rebuy units to trigger this multiple times.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution Requirements: Orehimi (Faith)">

To upgrade the standard Orehimi into her **Faith** form, players must collect specific evolution materials. This process significantly improves her range and the number of allies she can support.

**Evolution Materials Checklist:**
- **Hair Ornament** (x12): Obtained from the Kuinshi Palace.
- **Green Essence** (x30): Common material from challenges and shops.
- **Blue Essence** (x10): Rare material from legend stages.
- **Purple Essence** (x10): Rare material from crafting.
- **Pink Essence** (x20): Rare material from daily rewards.
- **Rainbow Essence** (x2): Mythic material required for high-tier evolutions.
- **Gold** (15,000): Standard currency for the evolution process.
