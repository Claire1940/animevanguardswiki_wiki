<Section icon={Target} title="Trait Value & Reroll Pity Rules">

Traits act as permanent modifiers that directly multiply a unit's combat statistics, such as Damage, SPA (Seconds Per Attack), and Range. Rerolling traits at the Mandra Traits NPC is a primary method for maximizing a unit's worth on the **anime vanguards value list**. 

Understanding the trait roll pool and its associated pity thresholds prevents players from wasting rare Trait Rerolls on low-tier modifiers.

| Trait Name | Roll Chance | Primary Role | Value Rating |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| Monarch | 0.1% | Single-placement carries | Excellent |
| Ethereal | 0.175% | Major carry units | Excellent |

Mythic traits feature robust pity mechanics. This ensures that players who commit to long reroll sessions will eventually secure a top-tier modifier. Pity thresholds are tracked individually for each unit and persist through evolution and stat transfers.

| Mythic Trait | Pity Threshold | Key Benefit |
|--- |--- |--- |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Evolution & Ascension Strategy">

Evolving and ascending your units are the final steps in maximizing their placement on the **anime vanguards value list**. Evolution completely transforms a unit's kit, often adding new attacks, wider AoE shapes, and powerful passive traits. Ascension, handled by the Kokashi NPC, provides raw percentage stat boosts across three distinct tiers.

| Ascension Level | Stat Bonus Granted | Target Priority |
|--- |--- |--- |
| Ascension I | Damage +3% | Evolved Mythic Carries |
| Ascension II | Range +5% | AoE and Control Units |
| Ascension III | Damage +7% | SSS-Tier Meta Cores |

To ascend a unit, you must sacrifice duplicate copies of that unit along with specific upgrade materials. This makes ascension an expensive, endgame-only system.
