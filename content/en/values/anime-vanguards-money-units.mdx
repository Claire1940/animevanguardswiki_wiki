#### Essential Traits for Farm Units

Unlike DPS units that thrive on traits like *Monarch* or *Demonic*, farm units rely heavily on utility-focused traits:
- **Fortune**: This is the absolute best trait for any farm unit. It provides a direct multiplier to all Yen generated per wave and slightly reduces the initial placement cost.

Acquiring these traits requires utilizing the game's Trait Reroll system. Players can farm thousands of reroll tickets by completing Odyssey proficiencies and climbing the Elemental Towers. Once you have a healthy stockpile of rerolls, prioritize rolling *Fortune* on your main farm units before investing in combat traits for your carries.
